About

I am a PhD student at LMU Munich in the Media Informatics Group. My research explores how personalization can be leveraged for privacy and security interventions—without introducing new privacy risks. I am particularly interested in privacy-preserving technologies that ensure adaptive, user-centric solutions remain secure and respectful of individual autonomy.

  • CyberFrame: AI-Supported Hybrid Comics for Cybersecurity Awareness Katharina Barlage, Moritz Will, Oliver Hein, Lukas Mecke, Philipp Thalhammer, and Florian Alt. 2026. In Proceedings of the Extended Abstracts of the CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI EA ’26). ACM, New York, NY, USA. CHI EA 2026
